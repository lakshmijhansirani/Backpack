Day 1: Arrival in Kochi

- Arrive at Cochin International Airport or Ernakulam Junction Railway Station.
- Check-in at a budget hotel in Kochi. Some budget-friendly options include Hotel Elite Palazzo, Hotel Abad Plaza, and Fort Muziris.
- Explore Fort Kochi, visit the iconic Chinese Fishing Nets, and take a stroll through the charming streets with colonial architecture.

Day 2: Arrival in Munnar

-Arrive in Munnar and check in to the hotel.
-Visit Tea Museum
-Visit the Eravikulam National Park
-Visit Mattupetty Dam and Lake.

Day 3:Munnar to Thekkady.

-Visit Mangala Devi Temple.
-Visit Elephant Safari

Day 4:Thekkady to Alleppey

-Visit St. Mary Forane Church, Champakulam.
-Oberon Mall, Kochi.
-Marari Beach.
