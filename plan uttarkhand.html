Day 1: Arrival in Nainital

- Arrive at Kathgodam Railway Station, the nearest railhead to Nainital.
- Check-in at a budget hotel in Nainital. Some budget-friendly options include Hotel New Bharat, Hotel Himalaya
- Explore the Naini Lake and enjoy a peaceful boat ride.

Day 2: Nainital Sightseeing

- Take a day trip to nearby attractions like Bhimtal, Naukuchiatal, and Sattal.
- Visit Naina Devi Temple and Hanuman Garhi for a spiritual experience.
- Enjoy the panoramic views from Tiffin Top (Dorothy's Seat) and Land's End.
- Return to Nainital for a budget-friendly dinner.

Day 3: Travel to Rishikesh

- Check out from the hotel in Nainital and travel to Rishikesh by bus or taxi. The journey takes around 5-6 hours.
- Check-in at a budget hotel in Rishikesh. Some budget-friendly options include Hotel Laxmi Palace, Hotel The Holiday Home.
- Explore the famous Lakshman Jhula and Ram Jhula suspension bridges.

Day 4: Rishikesh Adventure and Departure

- Start the day with an adventure activity like white-water rafting on the Ganges.
- Visit the Beatles Ashram, now known as Maharishi Mahesh Yogi Ashram.
- If you have extra time, explore Neelkanth Mahadev Temple or the nearby Rajaji National Park.
- Depart from Rishikesh and head to your next destination.
