Day 1:Arrival in Jaipur

- Arrival at Jaipur Airport or Railway Station.
- Some budget-friendly hotels in Jaipur include Hotel Sarang Palace, Hotel Ratnawali, and Hotel Anuraag Villa.
- After settling in, visit the Hawa Mahal, City Palace, and Jantar Mantar, which are all located close to each other.

Day 2: Jaipur Sightseeing

- Start the day with a visit to the Amber Fort. You can take an affordable local bus or hire a shared auto-rickshaw to reach the fort.
- After visiting the fort, explore the nearby Jaigarh Fort and Nahargarh Fort, which offer panoramic views of the city.

Day 3:Travel to Udaipur

- Check out from the hotel in Jaipur and travel to Udaipur. You can take a budget train or a sleeper bus for an economical journey.
- After arriving in Udaipur, check-in at a budget hotel like Panna Vilas, Hotel Rangniwas Palace, or Hotel Udai Palace.

Day 4:Udaipur Sightseeing and Departure

- Start the day with a boat ride on Lake Pichola, which offers picturesque views of Udaipur's palaces and surroundings.
- Visit Fateh Sagar Lake and Sajjangarh Palace (Monsoon Palace) for more beautiful views.
- For a budget lunch, explore the local eateries near Jagdish Temple or Bapu Bazaar.
