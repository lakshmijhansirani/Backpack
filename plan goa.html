Day 1: Arrival in Goa

- Check-in to a budget-friendly hotel for the entire duration of your stay. Budget hotels and guest houses can be found in areas like Calangute, Baga, or Anjuna.
- After settling in, head to the nearby beach for a relaxing afternoon. Calangute Beach is a popular option, but you can explore other less crowded beaches like Arambol or Morjim.
- In the evening, visit the Calangute Market or Anjuna Flea Market for some budget shopping and delicious street food.

Day 2:Explore North Goa

- Rent a scooter or use public transportation to explore North Goa's popular spots.
- Visit Aguada Fort for panoramic views of the Arabian Sea.
- Explore the vibrant and artsy streets of Anjuna.
- Head to Vagator Beach to witness a stunning sunset.

Day 3: Visit South Goa

- Travel to South Goa using buses or cabs (public transportation is cheaper).
- Relax on Colva Beach, which is one of the less crowded beaches in South Goa.
- Explore the scenic Palolem Beach and take a boat ride to Butterfly Island.

Day 4: Leisure and Return

- Spend the morning at leisure, relaxing on the beach or exploring local markets.
- Checkout from the hotel.
- If you have time, visit the Basilica of Bom Jesus in Old Goa and experience the rich history of the region.
- Depart from Goa, taking back wonderful memories.
