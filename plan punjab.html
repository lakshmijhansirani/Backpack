Day 1: Arrival in Amritsar

-Arrive in Amritsar, the cultural and spiritual heart of Punjab.
-Visit the iconic Golden Temple
-Explore Jallianwala Bagh.

Day 2: Amritsar Sightseeing

- Visit the Durgiana Temple
- Explore the Maharaja Ranjit Singh Museum
- Taste the famous Amritsari Kulcha and Chole.

Day 3: Patiala Exploration

- Travel to Patiala
- Visit the Qila Mubarak Complex
- Explore the local markets
- Enjoy the vibrant nightlife and entertainment in Patiala.

Day 4: Chandigarh and Departure

- Visit the famous Rock Garden
- Explore the beautiful Rose Garden
- Visit Sukhna Lake
