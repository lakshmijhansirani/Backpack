Day 1: Arrival in Visakhapatnam

-Arrive in Visakhapatnam and check in at a nearby hotel.
- Visit Rama Krishna Beach, popularly known as RK Beach.
- Explore Kailasagiri Hill Park and take a cable car ride for panoramic views
- Visit Rushikonda Beach for water sports and relaxation.

Day 2: Borra Caves and Araku Valley

- depart for Borra Caves, around 90 km from Visakhapatnam.
- Explore the fascinating caves and enjoy the natural beauty.
- continue your journey to Araku Valley
- Visit the Tribal Museum.

Day 3: Vijayawada and Amaravati

- Travel to Vijayawada and visit the Kanaka Durga Temple
- Explore the Undavalli Caves
- visit the Amaravati Stupa and the Archaeological Museum.

Day 4: Tirupati and Departure

- visit the Sri Venkateswara Temple
- You can do some last-minute shopping or visit any nearby attractions.
