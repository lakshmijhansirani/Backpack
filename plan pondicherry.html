Day 1: Arrival in Pondicherry

-Check-in at a budget-friendly hotel or guesthouse in the city center.
-Explore the French Quarter (White Town) with its colonial-style buildings and picturesque streets.
-Visit Aurobindo Ashram to experience its tranquil ambiance and learn about Sri Aurobindo .

Day 2: Beaches and Auroville

-Spend the morning at the famous Promenade Beach and enjoy the sea breeze.
-Visit Auroville, an experimental township, and explore the Matrimandir
-Enjoy a serene evening at Serenity Beach or Auroville Beach.

Day 3: Chunnambar Boat House and Local Exploration

-Visit the Chunnambar Boat House and take a boat ride to Paradise Beach .
-Spend some leisure time at the beach and indulge in water sports if interested.
-Explore local boutiques and shops for handicrafts, clothing, and souvenirs in the city.

Day 4: Pondicherry Museum and Departure

-Visit the Pondicherry Museum to learn about the region's history and art.
-Explore the Botanical Gardens for a peaceful time amidst nature.
-Depart from Pondicherry with fond memories.
