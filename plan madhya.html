Day 1: Arrival in Bhopal

-Arrive in Bhopal, the capital city of Madhya Pradesh.
-Visit the iconic Taj-ul-Masjid
-Explore the Upper Lake and enjoy a relaxing boat ride.
-Visit the Birla Museum and the Bharat Bhavan.

Day 2: Bhopal to Sanchi and Bhimbetka

-Take a day trip to Sanchi
-Explore the Great Stupa and other stupas, monasteries, and Ashoka Pillar.
-Visit Bhimbetka, a UNESCO World Heritage Site

Day 3: Bhopal to Khajuraho

-Travel to Khajuraho, a city known for its exquisite temples
-Visit the Khajuraho Group of Monuments
-Explore the Khajuraho Archaeological Museum.

Day 4: Khajuraho to Orchha and Departure

-Take a day trip to Orchha, a historic town with beautiful palaces and temples.
-Visit Orchha Fort, Jahangir Mahal, and Raj Mahal
-Explore Ram Raja Temple and Chaturbhuj Temple.
