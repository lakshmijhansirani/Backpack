Day 1: Arrival in Shimla

- Arrive at Shimla by bus or train and check-in at a budget hotel.
- Explore the Mall Road, Ridge, and Christ Church in Shimla.
- For budget dining, try local eateries and dhabas in and around Mall Road.

Day 2: Shimla Sightseeing

- Take a day trip to Kufri, enjoy horse riding or yak riding and take in the scenic views.
- Visit Jakhoo Hill and the Jakhoo Temple, beautiful views of Shimla.
- Visit the State Museum and learn about Himachal Pradesh's culture and history.

Day 3: Travel to Manali

- Check out from the hotel in Shimla and travel to Manali by bus or private taxi.
- Check-in at a budget hotel in Manali, include Hotel New Adarsh, Hotel Surabhi, and Hotel Ocean Blue.
- Explore the local markets in Manali, like Old Manali and Manu Market.
- For budget dining, try local cafes and restaurants serving Himachali cuisine.

Day 4: Manali Adventure and Departure

- Start the day with an early visit to Solang Valley for adventure activities like paragliding and zorbing.
- Visit Hadimba Temple and Vashisht Temple, known for their unique architecture and spiritual significance.
- If you have extra time, explore Jogini Falls or take a walk to the nearby villages.
- Depart from Manali and head to your next destination.
