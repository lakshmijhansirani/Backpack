Day 1: Arrival in Mumbai

- Arrive at Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport or Mumbai Central Railway Station.
- Check-in at a budget hotel in Mumbai. Some budget-friendly options include Hotel Traveller's Inn.
- Explore iconic landmarks like the Gateway of India, Marine Drive, and Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus.

Day 2: Mumbai Sightseeing

- Take a local train or taxi to explore Elephanta Caves, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
- Visit the Siddhivinayak Temple and Haji Ali Dargah for a spiritual experience.
- Explore the Bandra-Worli Sea Link and Bandra Fort for scenic views of the city.

Day 3: Travel to Lonavala/Khandala

- Check out from the hotel in Mumbai and travel to Lonavala/Khandala by train or bus.
- Check-in at a budget hotel in Lonavala or Khandala. Some budget-friendly options include Hotel Chandralok.
- Explore natural attractions like Lonavala Lake, Bhushi Dam, and Tiger's Leap.

Day 4: Lonavala/Khandala Adventure and Departure

- Start the day with an adventure activity like trekking to places like Duke's Nose or Rajmachi Fort.
- Visit Karla Caves, a historical Buddhist site near Lonavala.
- If you have extra time, visit the nearby hill station of Khandala and explore its viewpoints. - Depart from Lonavala/Khandala and head to your next destination.
