Day 1: Arrival in Chennai

- Arrive at Chennai International Airport or Chennai Central Railway Station.
- Check-in at a budget hotel in Chennai. Some budget-friendly options include Hotel Pearl International, Hotel Greens Gate.
- Explore the Marina Beach, the second-longest urban beach in the world.

Day 2: Chennai Sightseeing

- Take a day trip to Mahabalipuram, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, to explore ancient temples.
- Visit the beautiful Kapaleeshwarar Temple in Mylapore.
- Explore the bustling streets of Pondy Bazaar and T. Nagar for shopping and street food.
- Return to Chennai for a budget-friendly dinner.

Day 3: Travel to Pondicherry

- Check out from the hotel in Chennai and travel to Pondicherry ,The journey takes around 3-4 hours.
- Check-in at a budget hotel in Pondicherry. Some budget-friendly options include Hotel Corbelli.
- Explore the French Quarter of Pondicherry with its colonial architecture and vibrant streets.
- Visit the Promenade Beach and enjoy the serene views of the Bay of Bengal.

Day 4: Pondicherry Adventure and Departure

- Start the day with an adventure activity like water sports at Serenity Beach or Chunnambar Boat House.
- Visit Auroville, an international community promoting sustainable living and spiritual practices.
- Explore the Aurobindo Ashram and its peaceful surroundings.
- Depart from Pondicherry and head to your next destination.
