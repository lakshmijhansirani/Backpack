Day 1: Ahmedabad

-Arrive in Ahmedabad, Gujarat's largest city, and check into a hotel.
-Visit Sabarmati Ashram, a historic site associated with Mahatma Gandhi.
-Explore the heritage sites of Ahmedabad, including the Adalaj Stepwell and Sarkhej Roza.
-Visit Kankaria Lake and enjoy the various activities it offers.

Day 2: Vadodara

-Statue of Unity and Vadodara
-Early morning, depart for the Statue of Unity, the world's tallest statue.
-visit the Sardar Sarovar Dam and enjoy the scenic views of the Narmada River.

Day 3: Rann of Kutch and Departure

-morning sunrise and evening sunset in rann of kutch.
-Drive to the Great Rann of Kutch, a salt desert with breathtaking landscapes.

Day 4:Afternoon departure

-The Gateway Hotel Akota Gardens
-Visit Kankaria Lake and enjoy the various activities it offers.
